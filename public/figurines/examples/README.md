# Figurine Example Photos

Place your example photos here with these exact names:

## Required Photos:

1. **digital-design.jpg** 
   - The photo from your car showing the iPad with the digital 3D model
   - Crop to focus on the iPad screen showing the character design
   - Recommended size: 1200x900px

2. **printed-figurine.jpg**
   - The photo of the printed beige/skin-colored figurine
   - Crop to focus on the figurine, clean background
   - Recommended size: 800x1200px (vertical)

3. **painted-final.jpg** (if you have one)
   - Final painted figurine
   - Crop to focus on the figurine
   - Recommended size: 800x1200px (vertical)

## How to crop:

1. Open photos on your phone/computer
2. Use built-in photo editor to crop
3. Focus on the figurine/design, remove extra background
4. Save as JPG
5. Place in this folder with exact names above
